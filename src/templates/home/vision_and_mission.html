Vision and Mission

Vision   


“To be globally acclaimed Institute in Technical Education and rural technological Research that provides a transformative education to create innovators and leaders that serves for the betterment of the society and humanity at large”.

Mission   


  • To impart quality education with values and equip students to learn and apply technology in service to humanity.
  • To build and maintain world-class infrastructure and adopt modern automation technologies for the purpose of organizational efficiency.
  • To design the education through a continuous process so that the students qualifying from the institute have the top rating in placement.
  • To create an ambiance for healthy teaching-learning process and initiate research & developments.
