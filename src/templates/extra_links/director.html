Dr. Vimal Mishra

Dr. Vimal Mishra obtained his Ph.D. degree in Computer Engineering from Department of Computer Science & Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology- BHU (I.I.T.-BHU), Varanasi and M. Tech. in Electronics Engineering with specialization in Digital Electronics & Systems and B. Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering from Kamla Nehru Institute of Technology, Sultanpur. His research interests are machine translation, natural language processing, artificial intelligence, intelligent web, and cloud computing. He has published several books and research articles in national and international journals of repute. He has been working on leadership roles in various Govt. institutes since last 30 years in technical education. He has been serving as a director in IERT , Allahabad since May, 2014.
