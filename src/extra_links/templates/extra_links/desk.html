Director's Desk


Welcome to the Institute of Engineering and Rural Technology (IERT), Allahabad. Since its formation, IERT has been serving as our nation’s flagship as comprehensive institution of technical education. We are advancing institute’s tradition of excellence in Engineering and Technology through high-quality programs in teaching, research, executive training, and service. We continue to rank among the nation’s best Engineering Colleges.
Our goal is to prepare experts, who go beyond conventional technical skills. In order to meet the needs of the fast changing 21st century world, IERT is producing high-quality engineers of international standard. The institute not only provides high technology-based education to students but also lays emphasis on the cultivation of modern outlook. The institute also provides a congenial atmosphere for students to facilitate their development as a balanced and mature human being as they grow in life.
We are fortunate to have a talented, highly committed teaching and support staff here to ensure the learning environment of our students. Our faculties are renowned scholars and accomplished practitioners who are actively engaged in the academic excellence and innovative research ideas of the modern world.
We seek to prepare our young students with the very best preparation for life at IERT. We also seek to impart in our students a passion for learning that will bring the knowledge and understanding required for a positive contribution to the community.

Dr. Vimal Mishra
   (Director I.E.R.T.)
